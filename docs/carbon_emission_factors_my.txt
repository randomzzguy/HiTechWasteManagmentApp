MALAYSIA CARBON EMISSION FACTORS
Reference Document for Carbon Accounting

ENERGY EMISSION FACTORS (2022):
Grid electricity: 0.694 kg CO2e/kWh (Peninsular Malaysia)
Grid electricity: 0.553 kg CO2e/kWh (Sabah)
Grid electricity: 0.474 kg CO2e/kWh (Sarawak)
Source: Sustainable Energy Development Authority (SEDA) Malaysia

TRANSPORT EMISSION FACTORS:
Diesel (road transport): 2.68 kg CO2e/litre
Petrol (road transport): 2.31 kg CO2e/litre
CNG (compressed natural gas): 2.04 kg CO2e/kg
Source: IPCC AR6, Malaysia Energy Commission

WASTE EMISSION FACTORS:
Municipal solid waste to landfill: 467 kg CO2e/tonne (Malaysia, tropical)
Food waste to landfill: 520 kg CO2e/tonne (high methane generation)
Paper to landfill: 380 kg CO2e/tonne
Plastic to landfill: 50 kg CO2e/tonne (low biodegradability)
Source: IPCC 2006 Guidelines, Malaysia-specific conditions
