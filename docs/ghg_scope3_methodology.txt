GHG PROTOCOL - SCOPE 3 CATEGORY 5: WASTE GENERATED IN OPERATIONS
Methodology Guide for Hi-Tech Waste Management Sdn. Bhd.

OVERVIEW:
Scope 3 Category 5 covers emissions from third-party disposal and treatment of waste generated in a company's operations.
This includes solid waste, wastewater, and hazardous waste disposal.

MALAYSIA-SPECIFIC EMISSION FACTORS:

1. TRANSPORT EMISSIONS
Diesel fuel emission factor: 2.68 kg CO2e per litre
Source: IPCC AR6, Malaysia Energy Commission 2023
Formula: Transport emissions = fuel consumed (L) x 2.68 kg CO2e/L

2. LANDFILL AVOIDANCE CREDIT
Malaysia landfill methane emission factor: 467 kg CO2e per tonne of waste diverted
Source: IPCC 2006 Guidelines, Malaysia landfill conditions (tropical climate, high moisture)
Formula: Landfill avoidance = diverted waste (tonnes) x 467 kg CO2e/tonne

3. RECYCLING CREDITS (WRAP Methodology)
Paper/Cardboard: 700 kg CO2e saved per tonne recycled
PET Plastic: 1,500 kg CO2e saved per tonne recycled
HDPE Plastic: 1,400 kg CO2e saved per tonne recycled
Aluminium: 9,500 kg CO2e saved per tonne recycled (high energy savings)
Steel/Ferrous: 1,500 kg CO2e saved per tonne recycled
Glass: 300 kg CO2e saved per tonne recycled
Electronic waste: 2,000 kg CO2e saved per tonne recycled (estimated)
Source: WRAP Material Change for a Better Environment, 2023

4. WASTE-TO-ENERGY CREDIT
Malaysia grid emission factor: 0.694 kg CO2e per kWh (2022)
WtE credit: 200 kg CO2e per tonne processed
Source: Malaysia Energy Commission, Sustainable Energy Development Authority (SEDA)

5. BSF BIOCONVERSION CREDIT
Food waste to BSF larvae: 550 kg CO2e per tonne of food waste diverted
Includes: Landfill methane avoidance + fishmeal substitution credit
Source: IPCC, FAO Life Cycle Assessment of insect protein production

NET CARBON IMPACT FORMULA:
Net CO2e = Transport Emissions - Landfill Avoidance - Recycling Credits - WtE Credits

A negative net value indicates a net environmental benefit.

DIVERSION RATE CALCULATION:
Diversion Rate (%) = (Recyclable kg + WtE kg + BSF kg) / Total Waste kg x 100
